Our Love Story

Chapter One

Where It All Began

It started the way the most beautiful things often do. Not with grand gestures or perfectly planned moments, but with something quiet and unhurried. Two people, the same church, and a God who clearly had a plan neither of them saw coming. There was something about Hosanna that Jason couldn't quite shake. And something about Jason that made Hosanna smile a little longer than she meant to.

Chapter Two

Two People Who Just Fit

Jason's gentleness is the kind that wraps around you quietly, making Hosanna feel safe, loved, and completely at home. He is her constant, her steady place to land, and her favourite person. He makes her laugh unlike anyone ever has. For Jason, Hosanna is nothing short of life-changing. She has drawn him closer to his calling, made him want to push himself, and shown him what it looks like to be truly known and loved. They don't just love each other — they bring out the best in each other, every single day.

Chapter Three

The Proposal

Jason had been quietly planning for nearly a year, sourcing the ring long before Hosanna had any idea. With the help of friends and family who deserve an award for keeping a secret this big, he pulled it off completely. She walked in with no idea what she was walking into. When the excitement took over, she missed Jason pouring his heart out. After an awkward pause she asked him to pop the question. He stared at her and said, “I DID!! Will you marry me?!” She said yes. And then they both burst out laughing — because of course that is exactly how their story would go.

Chapter Four

Forever Starts Here

Looking back, it is almost hard to believe how far they have come. And yet, neither of them can imagine a version of their story that ends any other way. From the very beginning, this was always end game. On the 8th of August 2026, surrounded by everyone who loves them, Jason and Hosanna will stand before God and make it official. Their story is a reminder that love is not just a feeling — it is a choice, made over and over again. He is the reason they have come this far, and the foundation of everything still to come.
